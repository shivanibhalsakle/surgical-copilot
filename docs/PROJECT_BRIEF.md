# Project brief

## Problem

Preoperative teams must estimate patient risk and anticipate postoperative resources before surgery. These decisions influence ICU reservations, monitoring plans, staffing, blood preparation, and bed capacity.

## Proposed product

INSPIRE Surgical Copilot is a research prototype that converts preoperative patient and procedure information into:

1. Risk estimates
2. Expected length of stay
3. Resource-intensity indicators
4. Explainable contributing factors

## Initial users

- Preoperative assessment clinicians
- Anesthesiology teams
- Surgical coordinators
- Bed-management and perioperative operations teams

## MVP boundaries

The MVP will not:

- Recommend a medication or treatment
- Replace clinical judgment
- generate definitive equipment orders
- use post-incision or postoperative information for preoperative predictions
- claim clinical readiness without external and prospective validation
